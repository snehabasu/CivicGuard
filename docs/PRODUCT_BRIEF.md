# Product Brief: CivicGuard

## Problem
Social workers spend a large portion of their day on documentation, reducing face-to-face support time and increasing burnout risk.

## Product Thesis
CivicGuard captures visit conversations, extracts clinically relevant details, and drafts compliance-aware outputs with minimal manual rework.

## Primary Users
- County/state social workers
- Case supervisors
- Agency compliance reviewers

## Core Jobs-To-Be-Done
- Record visit conversation (with consent)
- Generate concise case visit summary
- Draft SOAP note sections
- Flag high-stress indicators for follow-up
- Produce filing-ready structured output

## MVP Demo Scope
- Input: one simulated 15-second audio clip
- Processing: mock transcription and note generation pipeline
- Output:
  - Case Visit Summary
  - SOAP note draft
  - High-Stress keyword flags

## Out of Scope (for now)
- Production-grade EHR integrations
- Multi-tenant billing logic
- Full policy engine by jurisdiction
